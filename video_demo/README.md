# Demo Video

This folder contains a short demonstration video of the **Retail Demand Forecast & Inventory Assistant**.

## Video Details
- **File**: `demo_video.mp4`
- **Language**: English (with clear narration)

## What the video shows:
- How to upload your sales CSV file
- Adjusting settings (Risk Level, Cash Flow Cycle, Lead Time, Forecast Horizon)
- Viewing the Forecast Overview tab (predictions and reorder suggestions)
- Exploring the Business Insights section (weekly patterns, promotion effects, etc.)
- Downloading the forecast result as CSV
